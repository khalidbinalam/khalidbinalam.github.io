About

Electronic music production with system-level clarity

Khalid Bin Alam, also known as অপূর্ব (Apurba), is an independent electronic music producer and audio engineer shaping ambient, downtempo, experimental, and club music. His work combines creative depth, technical precision, and independent infrastructure for long-term artist growth.

Identity
  • Producer & Engineer: Full-cycle music creation, sound design, mixing, and mastering.
  • Multi-genre Artist: Releases spanning ambient, experimental, downtempo, and melodic electronic music.
  • Curator & Builder: PlayLSD discovery, playlist strategy, and artist ecosystem development.
Approach

The practice is built on systems thinking rather than one-off releases. Creative integrity, engineering discipline, and strategic infrastructure combine to support sustainable artist projects.

  • Sound should serve emotional space and clarity.
  • Music is designed for long-form listening and performance.
  • Discovery and release systems are built in parallel.
Experience
  • Self-directed, independent career growth.
  • Hands-on expertise in music production and audio engineering.
  • Practical knowledge of digital distribution and release strategy.
  • Artist development through playlists and curated ecosystems.
Next steps

The long-term direction is to evolve from producer into a catalog-centric ecosystem leader, where music creation, engineering, and discovery reinforce each other.
